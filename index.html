About Project 100

Project 100 is an ambitious initiative aimed at transforming Uganda over the next 100 years. Our goal is to foster sustainable development, empower communities, and create a prosperous future for all Ugandans.

Inspiration

The inspiration behind Project 100 comes from the vision of a better future for Uganda. We are motivated by the wisdom and determination of great leaders who have inspired change across the world.

"The time is always right to do what is right." - Martin Luther King Jr.
